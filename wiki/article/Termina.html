Termina was discovered by Max Velocity, a Bozo admiral before The Dabbling. He failed to make diplomatic deals with them and through his visits he took readings of the planet to determine that Sanna was the only living thing there besides the Termina#Moon. He tried to cloak her away, but failed.

They came back a hundred years later to get Sanna out of there, and the Max Velocity family visited through it's generations up to Max Velocity Xtreme who now oversees the destruction of the moon. The plan began with freeing Sanna, but by the present day, selfish resource hoarding has taken over, as The Elven Conspiracy needs moon minerals to produce I-Me.
