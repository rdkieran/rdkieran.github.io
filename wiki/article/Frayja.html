Frayja

Depiction

A Thread from The Spool. Daughter of Bobbin and sister of Sewella Ty, and Fred

Frayja is an elusive character. She was the first Thread to leave The Spool, and met with exploitation at the hands of The Collective, and Siana.

Her clones were sent unwittingly into the universe to collect information for The Collective. Her clones can be found on board Beep Boop 4, USS Voyeur.

The universe hasn't noticed her yet. She's just a unique alien who shows up in lots of places, and never seems to remember you. But she's kind, so she's unwittingly good at brushing off these kind of coincidences as a lapse in her own memory.

Ewework

One of Frayja worked at Big Business#Ewework with Benny, Siobhan, Pratt, Gary, Ihava Fullbody, and Ihava's dog, Fido. There, she researched regenerative biology using her own biology and the research of Ihava Fullbody, who was only an arm when they met.

Evil Frayja

Siana created Evil Frayja to keep a genetic sample of Frayja close to hand in case it was ever needed. She is manipulated to be evil. Frayja, and all Threads, are easily manipulated, as their personalities are formed based on the clothes they wear. Evil Frayja got a monocle when she was born, which influenced her greatly.
