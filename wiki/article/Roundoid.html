Description

The Rind of Roundoids are a member species of The Galactic Federation of Planets from The Peel, an unraveled planet latched onto the Snerson home world S.

Roundoids typically have plus-shaped eyes, allowing them to see things the right way up even when their heads are tilted to the side.
