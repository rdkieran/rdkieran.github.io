Species: Shitter

Siana is a genetic engineer working with The Collective, with secret allegiance to The Elven Conspiracy, working through the elusive Sir, betraying Brenda, The Collective's true founder.

As a genetic engineer her speciality is behavioural genetics. She used these skills to clone Frayja, creating variants on her to act as spies across the universe for The Collective.

She's a good friend to Frayja, which she relies on to keep the clones reporting back.

She's separated from her homeworld of Drain#Shit, and her ties there. She doesn't know that Shit has been Flushed down The Drain. I don't know how she'll feel when she finds out.
