The Bombast of Bozo are the founding species of The Galactic Federation of Planets from the planet Majoris Maximus in the Bozotopia system.

The Bozo have a longstanding relationship with the Coil, who they consider to be a subspecies of Bozo.

Culture

The Bozo are the original winners. All of the space adventures there are to have, they had them first. They charted the stars before anyone else, they founded the Federation, and they hold all major positions of influence in it.

The Bozos are comfortable. Their society is naively simple, and it seems to work with no effort whatsoever.

Biology

Bozo are peas. They're picked from trees. When two Bozos love each other very much, they go to this tree and they dance underneath it while all their friends gather around to celebrate them. The goodwill of the Bozo people pleases the plant, and it creates a new Bozo in the image of the happy couple. It is picked from the tree and traditionally delivered by Stork.
