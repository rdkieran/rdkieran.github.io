Stork Hologram

Depiction

Stork is is Stork-type Hologram working for the Printer Burger on Beep Boop 4, and owned by The Galactic Federation of Planets. He's under the command of Printer Burger coordinator, Kaycee.

He has no officially assigned name, but Kaycee calls him Grommash or Grom, an old Troll name. He works cleaning bins and doesn't have any feelings about it whatsoever apparently.
