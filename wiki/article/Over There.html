Over There is the farthest frontier of Federation Space which juts into the side of Conspiracy Space. It's not got a lot going it, it's a bit of a no man's land, and politically unstable as well. Generally if you're sent there, you're likely being hazed by someone higher up than you.

As a borderland to Party Zone, it serves as a diplomatic starting point for Elves entering Federation Space, and those leaving for Conspiracy Space. Because of this, there is a lot of Barket activity, and a lot of corruption.
