Synopsis

The crew of the Stork pirate vessel Spacebeak is in a dire situation. They've lost weapons and are losing life support. Another wave of enemy forces from The Baddies is on its way to finish them off.

Feather, a young go-getting pirate is determined to save them, despite the wishes of Captain Birdbeard to activate the self-destruct sequence and kill themselves early. Not very honourable, if you ask Feather.

Through incompetence and bad luck, Feather fails to save the ship, and is returned through a kind of time loop by Life, who in their benevolence has allowed the crew a number of tries to save themselves, returning to the last minutes before the Spacebeak explodes.

Feather ultimately fails, and it's left to their last try, at which point Peck uses his hand of Mutiny! cards to devise a plan to save the day.
