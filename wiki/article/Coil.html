List of Coils

The Camaraderie of Coils are a member species of The Galactic Federation of Planets from the Tether, an asteroid belt extending behind the path of the desert planet Minor Minimus in the Bozotopia system.

The Bozo consider Coils to be a subspecies of Bozo; an assertion the Camaraderie of Coils strongly reject.
