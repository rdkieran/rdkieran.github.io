Dizbizazzle Bizzlebozzle 8 is the breakthrough fuel source manufactured by the Bozo. Say what you like about the Bozo; they benefitted from the generosity of many species to establish themselves as the prime species in The Galactic Federation of Planets hierarchy, but they manufactured Dizbizazzle Bizzlebozzle 8, and without it, the Federation would have never existed.

It was named for the two Bozos who discovered it; Blim Dizbizazzle and Blam Bizzlebozzle.

Together they worked on developing Nectar sweets from the sap of the Skytree rooted to The Woods#Too Big. Their methods developed the most potent Nectar of any Nectaratier. They marketed it as Blim Blam's Dizbizazzle Bizzlebozzle, and it was famous throughout the Bozotopia System. It was especially loved by The Bears, who had worked with nectar for thousands of years without fully exploring its deeper qualities.

As they developed more and more potent Nectar, they found it multiplied in uses each time. They began testing the nectar for more uses, and released new versions of their nectar with a new set of uses each time. It was for the 8th iteration that they discovered it's use as a fuel for a new propulsion system: The HESS Drive.

This was the most important development for Dizbizazzle Bizzlebozzle, and despite it's many uses, this became the use for which it is now most well known.

The development of Dizbizzazzle Bizzlebozzle allowed the Federation to expand into further star systems, eventually leading to it's broad influence it enjoyed later.
