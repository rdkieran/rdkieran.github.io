The Allspecies™, commonly known as "The Collective" or "Whatchimacallits" is a biologically adaptive hivemind race of Frankenstein's monsters.

Where Holograms are mentally adaptive with limited physiologically, Allspecies™ are biologically adaptive, with limited mental abilities.

It is named for The Collective, a small faction of scientists which created it, after their original aims were corrupted by the influence of The Elven Conspiracy, in particular Laelithar and his family.

The development of the Allspecies™ began as an exercise in reversing death by Triple philosopher Brenda, and Troll geneticist Hayley. They sought funding through The Barket to establish a base of operations on Party Zone. They enlisted the help of Bozo scientist Grundo, and others. Their funding came from Laelithar's family, working secretly within Party Zone to develop technology to advance their interests in Conspiracy Space.

The key genetic components that allow the Allspecies™ to do its thing are Beeple collective thought, Thread spontaneous generation, and regenerative genetic technology developed by the Human scientist Ihava Fullbody.
