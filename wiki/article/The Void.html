The Void is a vast expanse of black space in the Over There that no stars can penetrate. The only species known to have come from The Void are the Grue, a species of monsters who exist in pitch darkness. So far, they've proven to be quite nice and accommodating.

There seems to be some kind of relationship between The Void and Photonic Space that has caused a third anomaly, the Rainbow Deathspiral. The Rainbow Deathspiral is an arcing twist of space that descends from Photonic Space into The Void, in which the comfortable monochrome black of the universe begins to shift to what we understand as Hues, or Colour.

There are some research stations positioned out here to observe the phenomenon. The now defunct Inconspicuous IV was once used to house the Institute for Finding Things Out, but later hosted research facilities for The Collective.
