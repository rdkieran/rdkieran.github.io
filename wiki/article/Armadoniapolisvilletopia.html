Named by Bozo explorer Blubberbox Bambano, the Armado themselves had no name for the planet, typically referring to it with a respectful stomp on the ground.

The planet and it's people were discovered after the establishment of the Federation and the advent of interstellar travel. Despite being a neighbouring system to the central Bozotopia, Armadonia was unannounced in the universe, sending and receiving no messages with SpaceNet.

Despite their lack of communications, the Armado had well-developed internal communications, ignoring the outside universe.

The airwaves on Armadonia are kept clear, to maintain a consistent rhythm across the planets surface. Armados walk in step with one another, following a hum that resonates through the planet. Their affinity to rhythm bears similarity to the sensitivity of the Bozo.
