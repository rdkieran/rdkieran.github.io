Blam Bizzlebozzle

Depiction

Blam Bizzlebozzle was a Bozo chemist and nectaratier who, along with fellow chemist Blim Dizbizazzle, co-invented Dizbizazzle Bizzlebozzle 8.

Dizbizazzle Bizzlebozzle

Blim and Blam were chemists working with the Institute for Finding Things Out, a station that once orbited the skies above The Woods#Too Big as a science vessel at the dawn of The Federation. There they developed Dizbizazzle Bizzlebozzle 8 together, originally a sweet treat for Bears, made from the Nectar of the Skytree.
