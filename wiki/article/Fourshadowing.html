Fourshadowing is an off-gray psychic mist. Like the Darkness of Party Zone, but gray instead of black. A consciousness comprised of billions of resolved consciousnesses. Like midochlorians.

Fourshadowing leeches the continuity out of people. It draws out the memories of past selves, and all the lore people carry, and brings it to the surface.

It can erase memory, rearrange them at will. It happens to Garratand Teddie on Termina, as emitted by the Termina#Moon. It was used on Sanna to keep her young, and it can turn back the clock on everyone it influences, mentally and physically. It does not effect Photonic.
