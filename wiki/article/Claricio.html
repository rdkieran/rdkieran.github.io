Claricio is a Ceramic Construct serving the The Galactic Federation of Planets on board the USS Voyeur.

She was referenced by Rat Boy in one of his logs, asking whether his time Projection was in fact a Ghost, despite the fact that Ghosts do not exist.

She is highly superstitious, believing that any space phenomena is something silly like Ghosts or Werewolves when usually it's something mundane like an after image from an alternate reality or a standard lunar beast. She really wants ghosts to be real.
