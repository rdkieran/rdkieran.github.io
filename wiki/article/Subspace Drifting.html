Subspace Drifting is not yet a real story. It's an idea to develop a story inspired by racing movies, shows, and games. Fast and Furious, Redline, Speed Racer, Initial D, Racing Lagoon.

In space, high-speed travel is achieved by manipulating subspace collision planes. There has sprung up a thrillseeker culture for those who want to test their nerve and skills.

The form of this story may be a comic, it may not be. It will take place after the events of Space Story II The Wrath of Something and Loose Ends. It can pull from the inclusion of The Baddies in Self-Destruct Sequence. It could be used as a story for Sanna in a future Space Story entry, or it may be used as a Handa story.
