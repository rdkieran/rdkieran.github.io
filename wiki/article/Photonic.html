Photonics are like Holograms, but not. They don't like holograms, they consider holograms to be something like a doppelganger or a skin changer.

However, the The Royal Dominion of Heavenly Bodies seem to recognise them as being too alike to differentiate, and so Holograms of a certain sophistication are afforded souls.

Photonics are light itself. They fight with forces of Darkness and Gray Area. Fourshadowing is a phenomena that some entities can utilise.
