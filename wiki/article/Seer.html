Seers are type of Entity who takes on a physical form. They're pervasive throughout all space faring cultures as guides and friendly company. They are essentially Unaligned, but it could be argued they fulfill the interests of The Royal Dominion of Heavenly Bodies by guiding the fragile lives of mortals.

They have extra-dimensional vision and hearing. The sense they have for this isn't something we have a word for, which I know is kind of a cop out, sorry.
