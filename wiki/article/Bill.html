Bill is a Seer on board the USS Voyeur. He is romantically linked with Vod. Bill loved an anomaly.

Seers come from non-linear space. They can see thoughts and future and past and feelings. They just see right through everything else and speak directly to people's souls.

He is a chatterbox, but he talks inside people's heads, so he's kind of quiet too.

Seers immerse themselves in this slice of reality as a kind of pilgrimage or spiritual exercise. They stay there for a time and then return to their realm.

Bill has bonded with Vod due to their similar ages. Bill is a young Seer at only 400 years old, while Vod has about 400 years of memory, despite only being 2 years old.

They have a pretty strict code of conduct, the Seers. They're capable of omnipotence and non-linear cognizance. They have access to way more global permissions.
